# NutriGrow — Integrated Nutrition App for Mothers & Children (Prototype)

**Live prototype:** https://app-nutrigrow.netlify.app/

NutriGrow is a mobile app concept that helps pregnant women and parents of toddlers monitor nutrition, access nutritionist-designed meals, and receive health education — designed as a response to stunting prevention and food safety needs in Indonesia's national nutrition program (MBG).

This prototype was developed as part of a business case project at IPB University's School of Business, using a Design Thinking approach centered on user needs.

## Problem

Parents — especially pregnant women and caregivers of toddlers — often struggle to determine appropriate nutrition, lack reminders for daily nutrition tracking, have limited access to nutrition education, and have limited time to prepare properly balanced meals. These gaps contribute directly to stunting risk in children.

## Solution

NutriGrow integrates four things into one app: ready-to-eat nutritionally-standardized meals, monitoring of daily nutrition intake, nutritionist consultation access, and health/nutrition education — all personalized to the user's stage (pregnancy, MPASI 6–8 months, MPASI 9–11 months, toddler, or preschool-age).

## Key Features (Prototype)

- **Dashboard** — personalized overview showing growth tracking (weight/height charts), quick access to menu ordering, warteg search, and nutrition consultation
- **Menu Ordering** — browse and filter meals by category, view calorie/protein info per item, get standardized meal recommendations, and add items to a "Rantang Saya" (My Meal Box) cart
- **Checkout & Monitoring** — order summary with full nutrition breakdown (calories, protein, carbs, fat), multiple digital payment options, and order/nutrition history tracking

## Tech Stack

Built as a front-end prototype using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, hosted on Netlify. This is a UI/UX prototype demonstrating the intended user experience — it is not yet connected to a backend, real meal inventory, or a payment gateway.

## Status

🚧 **Prototype stage.** Core screens (dashboard, menu ordering, checkout, monitoring) are functional and clickable. Nutrition data and order history are simulated. Not yet connected to a live backend or real nutritionist/meal-provider network.
